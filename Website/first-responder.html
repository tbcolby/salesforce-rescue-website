What Is a First Responder?

First Responders are senior Salesforce experts who can take last-minute, on-demand projects of varying scale and complexity — from 90-minute emergency fixes to multi-week rescues.

The Work

  • Emergency data recovery & corruption fixes
  • Failed deployment recoveries
  • Integration crisis response
  • Automation & Flow loop disasters
  • Security & compliance emergencies
  • Architecture assessments under pressure

The Benefits

  • Set your own rates — premium pricing for emergency work
  • Complete flexibility — work when you want
  • High-impact work — be the hero when it matters
  • Elite network — work alongside top 1% talent
  • Variety — every project is different
  • Remote-first — work from anywhere

Are You a Match?

We're building a network of the best. Here's who we're looking for:

You're a Great Fit If:

  • • 15+ years of Salesforce experience across complex implementations
  • • Multiple Salesforce certifications (CTA, Architect, or Technical Architect preferred)
  • • You thrive under pressure and can diagnose issues rapidly
  • • Expert-level technical skills in data, automation, integrations, or security
  • • Comfortable working independently with minimal hand-holding
  • • Available for short-notice projects (same-day or next-day start)
  • • Strong communication skills with stressed stakeholders
  • • Track record of fixing complex problems in high-stakes environments

Probably Not a Fit If:

  • • You have less than 10 years of Salesforce experience
  • • You prefer predictable, scheduled work weeks in advance
  • • You're early in your Salesforce career (we need senior-level judgment)
  • • You need constant direction or support while working
  • • You're looking for full-time employment (this is project-based)
  • • You don't handle high-pressure situations well
